## HARDWARE
### Matrix LED Module
LTP-12100M-08 [https://akizukidenshi.com/goodsaffix/ltp12188m.pdf]

## Interface
### Pmod
FPGA Board Pmod |Matrix LED pin
Pmod1 | ROW(Anode)
Pmod2 | Column_red(Cathode red)
Pmod3 | CLR3
Pmod4 | RCLOCK
Pmod5 | Column_green(Cathode green)
Pmod6 | CLR1
Pmod7 | CLR2
Pmod8 | CLOCK
